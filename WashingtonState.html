Apple Capital of the World

Washington State Spokane Seattle Olympia
Washington state is well-known as the apple capital of the world. It is also home to many agricultural focused operations.
  • Apples
  • Cherries
  • Blueberries
  • Potatoes
  • Wheat
  • Onions
Washington isn't only known for its agriculture, however. Residents enjoy widely varying geography and weather conditions. Western Washington tends to have consistent cloud cover and rainfall, especially those next to Puget sound. Central and eastern Washington have a desert climate.
